Lasagna

This is a lasagna recipe for crazy lasagna. It's actually cake made from the shattered dreams of the unborn. It works similar to weeping angels, feeding on the lost lives of those that never were.

Ingredients

  • Onions, probably
  • Sauce of some sort
  • big, floppy noodles
  • Sasuage and beefies

Steps

  1. Make the noodles floppy
  2. Put some cheese in some sauce and stir the living shit out of it
  3. Cook the meat, if desired
  4. Stuff all the things in the floppy noodles and then jam it in the oven
  5. Set oven to 500F and bake for 4 - 5 hours
